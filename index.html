Seamless Integration with HSI Cameras

SpectralEdge is designed to work seamlessly with HSI cameras of different types, from snapshot mosaic cameras to line-scanning cameras. With SpectralEdge, you can easily connect and acquire images from your HSI camera in real time, without the need to write a single line of code.

Get rid of complex and time-consuming software development tasks and focus on your image analysis and classification setup.

Powerful Cloud-Based Model Training

SpectralEdge's powerful cloud-based machine learning capabilities allow you to easily create, train, and deploy models for image classification. You can upload images to the cloud, create a dataset, and train an out-of-the-box model with just a few clicks using simple ready-to-execute -yet fully customizable- Python notebooks.

Once your model is trained, you can easily download it back to your SpectralEdge device and use it for real-time monitoring and control. Harness the power of state-of-the-art machine learning and take HSI to the next level.

Real-Time Monitoring and Control

Unlock the potential of hyperspectral imaging to monitor processes in real-time and detect defects or deviations from the desired output, enabling significant improvements of the overall quality and efficiency of production processes.

SpectralEdge's real-time capabilities allow you to perform real-time monitoring and control in different applications, as diverse as food production, woodworking or metal processing.

Easy to deploy in the factory floor

Quick to connect and deploy, easy to use and integrate in the factory floor by design. Just plug it and start using it right away. A user-friendly interface makes it easy to navigate and control the device.

With SpectralEdge, you can start using hyperspectral imaging on a factory floor with minimal software training and setup time, allowing you to quickly and easily improve production processes.
